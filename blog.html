Mentalism

Do you ever thought of becoming a super hero? I have. This is not a super ability from which you can super hero but its not less than being super. Mentalism is used by Mentalists. A Mentalist is not a magician, he uses his mentalism to trick your mind. With mentalism a mentalist can manipulate your mind, can hypnotise you, can use teleknesis skills too to move or bend things and objects. And yes a Mentalist can read minds too!!

So this skill of mentalism is so fascinating, isn't it? You can also learn this skill and become a mentalist.
